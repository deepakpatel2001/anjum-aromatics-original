Welcome to Anjum Aromatics

For over four decades, Anjum Group has been a pioneer in crafting premium essential oils. With a perfect blend of expertise and tradition, we take pride in producing the finest sandalwood oils and a diverse range of essential oils sourced ethically from India, Indonesia, Australia, and Africa. Our range includes sandalwood oils and essential oils for perfumery, skincare, aromatherapy, and medicinal applications, meeting the needs of various industries. With an unwavering focus on quality, our products have earned trust and recognition across Europe, Southeast Asia, and the Middle East. In 2014, we expanded our vision by establishing Anjum Aromatics in Bangalore, with the goal of becoming a leading global supplier of essential oils. Today, our operations include a modern manufacturing unit in Indore SEZ, a branch in Hyderabad, and a global head office in Dubai, reinforcing our commitment to quality and innovation on a global scope.
